Upload

Design your own 3D model or download one from Thingiverse, then upload a copy here.

.stl format only, 10MiB limit.

Slice

We'll automatically slice your model into a set of instructions which a 3D printer can understand, then tell you how long your model will take to print and how many Ꭶ it will cost.

Head over to the slicing page to monitor slicing progress for your uploaded models.

Print

Once you're happy with the quoted printing cost, you can submit your sliced model for printing. We'll contact you when it's done.

Use the slicing page to see which models you can print, or head over to the printing page to keep an eye on your prints in progress.
